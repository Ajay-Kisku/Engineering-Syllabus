What is manufacturing process?
A manufacturing process is a series of steps or operations used to transform raw materials into finished products. It involves various physical, chemical, or mechanical procedures to alter the form, properties, or appearance of a material to meet specific design and quality standards.

Engineering Mechanics
What is Engineering Mechanics?
Engineering Mechanics is the branch of mechanics that deals with the study of forces and their effects on solid bodies. It is a fundamental subject for all branches of engineering that focuses on the behavior of objects under the action of forces, using principles of physics, and mathematical modeling.
